# Asset Inventory

## Overview

This asset inventory identifies and classifies the critical assets owned and managed by VORTEX SECURE. The purpose of this inventory is to establish visibility over enterprise assets, support risk management activities, improve security governance, and ensure alignment with the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0.

---

# Asset Classification

| Classification | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| Critical | Assets essential for business continuity and security operations. |
| High | Important operational assets that directly support business services. |
| Medium | Internal operational assets with moderate business impact. |
| Low | Public or non-sensitive assets with minimal security impact. |
